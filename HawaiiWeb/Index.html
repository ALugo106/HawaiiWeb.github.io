Hawaii

Hawaii, constituent state of the United States of America. Hawaii became the 50th U.S. state on August 21, 1959. Hawaii is a group of volcanic islands in the central Pacific Ocean.

Hawaii’s cultural milieu is the result of overlay after overlay of varied cultural groups. For many years the legacy of colonialism in Hawaii was among the factors that diminished and diluted Native Hawaiian culture. Beginning in the 1970s, however, a renaissance led to the vibrant expression of the islands’ traditional culture that is evident today in Hawaii.
